Core Technologies

Flexible Solar Panel

Mechanism: Uses advanced printed Perovskite solar cells (PSCs), which are flexible, lightweight, and highly efficient even in low-light conditions.

Key Feature: Unlike traditional silicon panels, these can wrap around the curved edges of the phone case. They harvest energy from both direct sunlight (outdoor) and artificial indoor lighting.

Efficiency: ~25% conversion efficiency.

Acoustic Energy Harvester

Mechanism: Utilizes piezoelectric nanofiber membranes (PVDF) that vibrate in response to sound waves.

Key Feature: Converts noise pollution, music playback, and your own voice during calls into electrical potential. The membranes are tuned to resonate with human speech frequencies (85-255 Hz).

Output: Generates μW to mW scale power to prolong standby time.

Piezoelectric Module

Mechanism: Embedded lead Zirconate Titanate (PZT) sensors generate voltage when mechanically stressed.

Key Feature: Harvests kinetic energy from everyday actions: walking with the phone in your pocket, placing it on a table, or tapping the screen.

Durability: Tested for over 1 million distinct impact cycles.

Thermoelectric Generator (TEG)

Mechanism: Operates on the Seebeck Effect, creating voltage from the temperature difference (ΔT) between two surfaces.

Key Feature: Uses the heat generated by the phone's processor during heavy tasks (gaming, video rendering) and your body heat against the cooler air to generate power, effectively cooling the phone while charging it.

Smart Power Management IC

The brain of ENERGYNEST. Raw energy from harvesters is volatile and fluctuates between 0.5V to 3V. Our custom PMIC performs three critical functions:

  • Boost Conversion: Steps up low voltages to a stable 5V required for Lithium-Ion battery charging.
  • Source Arbitration: Intelligently prioritizes the most active energy source in real-time.
  • Protection: Prevents overcharging, overheating, and reverse current flow, ensuring 100% battery safety.
